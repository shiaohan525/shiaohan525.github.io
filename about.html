JOHN YOUNG ENTERPRISE CO., LTD

Our company has designed and manufactured electro-coating (electro-deposition or E.D.) peripherals, turnkey systems and equipment since 1982. Our systems have been in Taiwan, Southeast Asia (Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam) and China. We also export high-tech ultrafiltration(U/F) systems and reverse osmosis D.I. water treatment systems for E.D. lines and other fields. More as follows.
